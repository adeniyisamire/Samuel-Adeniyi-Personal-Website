Role & Contributions

I serve on FRC Team 2976 as an active member of both the build and electronics teams. My contributions include:

  • Pneumatics routing and drivetrain support
  • Safety checks, wiring documentation, and troubleshooting

Impact

Working on team projects developed my teamwork, technical problem solving, and project planning skills—plus experience presenting engineering work at outreach events.
